# Task: F-0001 Global News Research

## Goal
Conduct research on global news from the last 24 hours across Politics, Technology, and Economy and produce a report.

## Requirements
1.  Research major global news events from the last 24 hours.
2.  Cover categories: Politics, Technology, and Economy.
3.  Include headline, 2-3 sentence summary, and source link for each item.
4.  Identify at least 5 significant events.
5.  Output report to `docs/research/F-0001-news-report.md`.
