# F-0001: Latest Global News Research

## Status
- [x] Draft
- [ ] Review
- [ ] Approved

## User Need
As a product team, we need to stay informed about the latest global news from the last 24 hours to identify potential trends, risks, and opportunities that might affect our operations or product direction.

## Requirements
1. The research must cover major global news events from the last 24 hours.
2. The research should include at least three categories: Politics, Technology, and Economy.
3. Each news item must include a headline, a brief summary (2-3 sentences), and a source link (if available).
4. The output must be a Markdown report in `docs/research/F-0001-news-report.md`.
5. The research must identify at least 5 significant global events.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] A report exists at `docs/research/F-0001-news-report.md`.
- [ ] The report contains at least 5 distinct news items.
- [ ] Each news item has a headline, summary, and source.
- [ ] News items are categorized into Politics, Technology, and Economy.
- [ ] All news items are verified to have occurred within the last 24 hours from the time of research.

## Dependencies
- Internet access for search tools.

## Notes
- Ensure a diverse range of sources are used to avoid bias.
- Focus on events with global significance rather than purely local news.
